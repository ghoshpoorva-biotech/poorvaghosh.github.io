About

I am a molecular biologist with deep experience in small molecule drug discovery for oncology and allergic disease. I lead cross functional projects from target validation to preclinical development and I care about well designed studies, clear decisions, and steady progress. My goal is to help teams turn complex problems into therapies that make a difference.
